Nutro Heist

Our Story

Nutro Heist was created to help everyone make smarter dietary choices by quickly scanning food labels and highlighting hidden ingredients. Our AI uses state-of-the-art OCR to read and analyze labels, flagging additives for easier, healthier decisions.

Our Mission

We believe food transparency should be quick and accessible. Our mission is to empower all people to decode packaged foods in seconds, on the go, every day.
